Varning Productions

Who We Are

Since 2006, VARNING PRODUCTIONS has been a key pillar of the underground music scene in Montreal, founded by Janick Langlais. Since then, it has organized over 200 shows and worked with more than 600 bands from all around the world.

We focus on connecting emerging bands with more established names, fostering collaborations, tours, and authentic experiences within punk, metal, and other alternative genres.

A Varning From Montreal Festival

Our annual event celebrates its 17th edition in 2025. It’s a vital platform for punk and metal underground culture, bringing together bands from many parts of the world. Dates for the next edition coming soon.

Booking and Tours

We organize tours for bands playing the festival and collaborate with promoters across North America and Europe. If you're a band, venue, or agent, get in touch!
